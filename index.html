About Game

A long time ago there lived a powerful wizard. He could cause rain and lightning, could become into a dragon and burn with fire, could hide in the forest, pretending to be a tree. He had 3 students: Lunar Wizard, Forest Fairy, Fire Wizard. Each of them inherited only a few of his magical abilities.

You have to choose one of three wizards. Each wizard has unique spells. Before you enter the arena, you can choose a location, choose your character with unique spells. Each wizard can attack the enemy with one of two spells or recover his health or get armor, becoming briefly invulnerable.

This game is a great pleasure. Playing this game, you develop your intellectual abilities, improve memory and become more confident.
